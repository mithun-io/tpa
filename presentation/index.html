TPA Insurance Claim System

Next-Generation Automated Claim Settlement & Fraud Detection Platform

1. Project Overview

A highly scalable Third-Party Administrator (TPA) platform designed to automate and streamline the lifecycle of healthcare insurance claims.

From customer submission and AI-powered document validation to fraud detection and instant settlement via Razorpay, our system ensures accuracy, speed, and transparency at every step of the insurance workflow.

9. Fraud Detection Flow

🚨 Anomaly Triggers

  • Amount Mismatch: Claimed amount significantly exceeds standard procedure costs.
  • Date Mismatch: Service dates conflict with policy coverage periods.
  • Duplicate Claim: Identical service claims submitted multiple times.
  • Suspicious Pattern: High frequency of claims from the same provider/patient in a short time.

12. Security Design

🔐 Authentication

Stateless JWT (JSON Web Tokens) are used for securing all API communications. Tokens include role claims for rapid validation without DB hits.

🛡️ Authorization

Strict Role-Based Access Control (RBAC). Routes and actions are guarded against unauthorized roles (Customer vs. Admin vs. Carrier) via Spring Security annotations.

📦 Data Isolation

Tenant-level logic isolation ensures Carriers only view claims assigned to them, and Customers only access their own Personally Identifiable Information (PII).
